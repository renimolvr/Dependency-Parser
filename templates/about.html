About the Project

The Malayalam Dependency Parser is a natural language processing tool designed to analyze the grammatical structure of Malayalam sentences. This project aims to provide a comprehensive solution for parsing Malayalam text and identifying the relationships between words.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance natural language processing capabilities for the Malayalam language, making it more accessible for researchers, developers, and language enthusiasts. By providing this tool, we hope to contribute to the preservation and technological advancement of the Malayalam language.

Our Vision

To pioneer innovative, accessible Malayalam NLP tools that enable seamless digital interaction and empower communities to preserve and advance their linguistic heritage. Drive advancements in machine translation, sentiment analysis, and AI-driven language tools to enrich Malayalam’s role in global technology and cultural expression.

Team

Our team consists of linguists, computer scientists, and Malayalam language experts who have collaborated to create this tool. We welcome contributions and feedback from the community.
